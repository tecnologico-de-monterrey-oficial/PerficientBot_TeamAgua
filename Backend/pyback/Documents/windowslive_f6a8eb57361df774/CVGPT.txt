
Jane Franklin is a prolific lecturer, researcher and author with over 10 years of experience teaching undergraduate and postgraduate courses. She has supervised 22 BA theses, 9 MA theses, and 2 PhD dissertations, and has edited and co-authored 3 monographs on contemporary trends in drama and performative arts. She has also published over 30 articles in peer-reviewed journals. Jane holds a PhD in Comparative Literature from the University of Chicago, an MA in English from the City University of New York, and a BA in American Studies from the University of Oxford.
